"Unity Circus is an Acro theater organization based in Rubavu, Rwanda, East Africa. It was established in 2018 as a performing group of six young acrobat These six finally founded Unity Circus, an organization that offers a diverse educational program through acrobatics, theater and dance for 130 childrens from low-income families in the rural area around the City of Gisenyi called Kanembwe and more than 343 childrens whom indirectly benefiting. In 2021, Unity Circus Sport was registered as a Non-Government Organization accredited by Rwanda Governance Board with the certificate of compliance N 0 1226RGB/NGO/OC/06/2023. It aims to raise Acrotheater artists that will create a positive living environment in society."

Vision

The great vision of Unity Circus Sport is “to create Acro-theatre artists that will create a positive living environment in different societies”.

Mission

Unity Circus is dedicated to providing a nurturing and creative environment where young people can access quality arts training, education, and social support,On the side of mission, this is “Acrotheatre for Change.

Values

Creativity. Professionalism. Collaboration. Accessibility.
