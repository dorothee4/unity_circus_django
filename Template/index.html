Who we are

Unity Circus is an Acro theater organization based in Rubavu, Rwanda, East Africa. It was established in 2018 as a performing group of six young acrobat These six finally founded Unity Circus, an organization that offers a diverse educational program through acrobatics, theater and dance for 130 childrens from low-income families in the rural area around the City of Gisenyi called Kanembwe and more than 343 childrens whom indirectly benefiting. In 2021, Unity Circus Sport was registered as a Non-Government Organization accredited by Rwanda Governance Board with the certificate of compliance
N 0 1226RGB/NGO/OC/06/2023. It aims to raise Acrotheater artists that will create a positive living environment in society.

Unity Circus and the Covid 19 Pandemic

Training children in circus /Acrotheatre

Many children of all ages from the rural areas around Gisenyi come to Unity Circus to have Circus training as their freetime activity and to express their feelings through acrotheatre.

Encouraging young artist to attend their schools

Many children in rural areas of Rwanda don't attend schools. The reasons are that they are not able to afford schoolfees and that they don't perform well so they loose motivation.
