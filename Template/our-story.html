,Unity cirus was a performing group known as Unit Acrobats. They were working in Gisenyi town and made of three friends with same background. These were: Nshuti Emmanuel, Abubakal Hakizimana and Justin Ntwali. In the time of 2021, the group of three has got the idea of shifting from Gisenyi town to go to Kanembwe in the village of people that were living to the mount of Rubavu. This area for sure, it was remote thanks to how it was living people whose lives were based on agribulture. While the population of Gisenyi arrived there, it was to hard for children and youth to live there. Some of them had mid-sets of living the village to return back in Gisenyi for begging, stealing and some girls went there for prostitution. The three guys were met by other talented artists namely Niyonsenga Justin, Baryaningwe Mohammed and Maendeleo Sylvestre who would like to join them for making a strong and educative circus. The target was to group all those children who would leave the village to the city. By the end of 2022, a new member Sibomana Rachid joined us to enforce training among the young children. It was another supporting staff that was welcomed to the workings of Circus in Kanembwe. In 2023, Unity Circus has changed the name to Unity Circus Sport as an organization generated at Kanembwe at Bushengo Village. It has the mission of “Acrotheatre for change” to all young children that participate to the center as well as all people located in the region. The Organization Unity Circus Sport began with 92 as children support organization including 42 girls and 50 boys. Today the organization has 130 children.
